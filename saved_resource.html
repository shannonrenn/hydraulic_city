My Work…

My research focuses on the political ecology of urban infrastructures, and the social and material relations that they entail. Through anthropological research, I study the ways in which natural resources and specific configurations of ‘the public’ are mobilized to assemble urban development and environmental projects in Mumbai.

 

 

 
I am currently working on a book manuscript based on fieldwork done in one of Mumbai’s many informal settlements.To get water, the urban poor
assemble their hydraulic infrastructure not only by mobilizing pumps, pipes, plumbers, and politicians, but also city engineers, friends and family. A study of the infrastructures of water, made through these anxious arrangements, I suggest, may provide new insights into how the city’s public system
is made.
